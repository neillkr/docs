Four signal layers. One view. Updated in real time.

## Signal layers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Mood Spectrum">
    The emotional composition of your community at a glance. Each mood is tracked by volume, percentage share, and trend direction — so Joy dropping from 47% to 38% over 7 days is immediately visible before it becomes a retention event.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Psychological States">
    What's underneath the moods. Bubble visualisation where size reflects prevalence and a live pulse indicates acceleration. Loneliness at 29% with a flat trend reads very differently from Loneliness at 29% and rising — this layer makes that distinction impossible to miss.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Emerging Topics">
    The specific conversation themes gaining or losing momentum across your community. "Feeling replaceable" trending up 8% this month. "Body image" declining 5%. These are the signals that tell you what content to create, what groups to seed, and what Kai arcs to prioritise.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Suggested Actions">
    Kai's interpretation of all three layers above, translated into concrete next steps for your team. Not alerts — recommendations. Prioritised by impact, colour-coded by urgency, written in plain language your whole team can act on.
  </Step>
</Steps>
