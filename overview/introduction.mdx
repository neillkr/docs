Affective insights refer to the deep understanding, measurement, and analysis of emotions, moods, and attitudes — what psychologists call **affect**. While standard analytics focus on cold hard facts (clicks, sessions, conversions), affective insights surface the human truth behind the data: why a person feels or behaves a certain way.

Kai Affective Intelligence applies this principle to your existing content streams — Slack messages, WordPress comments, survey responses, support tickets. Your content flows in. Kai Brain analyses it using the same emotional intelligence framework that powers Kai's AI companion. Aggregated, anonymised signals flow back out. No individual data is ever retained.

<Note>
  The trend is the product. A single week's snapshot is interesting. Six weeks of burnout rising steadily in your engineering channel while staying flat in marketing is actionable. Kai detects these patterns before they become crises — and tells you exactly what to do about them.
</Note>
